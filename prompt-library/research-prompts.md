# Research Prompt Library

## Purpose

These prompts are designed to help gather information, identify trends, and produce high-quality research.

Always verify important information using multiple sources.

---

# General Research Prompt

Act as a professional research analyst.

Research the following topic:

[TOPIC]

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Key Findings
3. Major Trends
4. Risks
5. Opportunities
6. Sources Used
7. Confidence Level for Each Finding

Present findings in a structured report.

---

# Beginner Topic Exploration

I know very little about the following topic:

[TOPIC]

Explain:

* What it is
* Why it matters
* Current trends
* Common terminology
* Important organizations or companies

Assume I am a college student with no prior knowledge.

---

# Industry Research Prompt

Act as an industry analyst.

Research the current state of:

[INDUSTRY]

Identify:

* Major companies
* Market trends
* Growth opportunities
* Risks
* Future outlook

Present findings in business-report format.

---

# Competitive Research Prompt

Research the following competitors:

[COMPANY 1]

[COMPANY 2]

[COMPANY 3]

Compare:

* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Market position
* Customer perception
* Competitive advantages

Create a comparison table.

---

# Source Verification Prompt

Review the following claim:

[CLAIM]

Identify:

* Supporting evidence
* Contradicting evidence
* Source quality
* Potential bias

Provide a confidence rating from 1-10.

---

# Research Summary Prompt

Summarize the following information.

Focus on:

* Most important facts
* Key trends
* Risks
* Opportunities

Limit summary to one page.
