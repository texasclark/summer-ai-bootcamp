# Analysis Prompt Library

## Purpose

Analysis turns information into insights.

Research answers the question:

"What happened?"

Analysis answers the question:

"What does it mean?"

---

# Business Analyst Prompt

Act as a business analyst.

Analyze the following information:

[INFORMATION]

Identify:

* Patterns
* Trends
* Opportunities
* Risks
* Recommended actions

Explain your reasoning.

---

# SWOT Analysis Prompt

Perform a SWOT analysis.

Topic:

[TOPIC]

Identify:

Strengths

Weaknesses

Opportunities

Threats

Present results in a table.

---

# Trend Analysis Prompt

Analyze the following trend:

[TREND]

Explain:

* Why it is happening
* Drivers behind growth
* Potential risks
* Future outlook
* Recommended actions

Provide supporting evidence.

---

# Root Cause Analysis Prompt

A problem has occurred:

[PROBLEM]

Identify:

* Possible causes
* Most likely cause
* Supporting evidence
* Potential solutions

Rank solutions by effectiveness.

---

# Data Analysis Prompt

Analyze the following data:

[DATA]

Identify:

* Patterns
* Outliers
* Trends
* Surprising findings

Explain the business implications.

---

# Customer Perspective Prompt

Act as a customer.

Review:

[PRODUCT OR SERVICE]

Explain:

* Positive aspects
* Negative aspects
* Likely objections
* Improvement opportunities

Provide detailed feedback.
