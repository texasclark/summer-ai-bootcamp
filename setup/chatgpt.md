# ChatGPT Setup Guide

ChatGPT is an AI assistant that can help with:

* Research
* Writing
* Summarization
* Brainstorming
* Planning
* Analysis

Throughout this bootcamp it will function as a productivity tool.

## Prompting Basics

Bad Prompt:

Tell me about business.

Better Prompt:

Act as a business analyst.

Explain three major business trends affecting retail companies in 2026.

Provide examples and sources.

---

## Framework for Better Prompts

Role

Task

Context

Output Format

Example:

Act as a research analyst.

Research current trends in K-Pop performance groups.

Identify five trends.

Provide evidence and confidence levels.

Present findings in a table.

---

## Verification Rule

Never assume AI is correct.

Always verify:

* Important facts
* Statistics
* Dates
* Sources

---

## First Exercise

Ask ChatGPT:

What are three ways AI can improve productivity for college students?

Evaluate the response.

Ask follow-up questions.
