# Claude Setup Guide

## What Is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant developed by
Anthropic.

Like ChatGPT, Claude can help with:

* Research
* Analysis
* Writing
* Planning
* Summarization

Different AI systems often produce different results.

Comparing outputs is an important skill.

---

## Step 1: Create an Account

Visit:

https://claude.ai

Create a free account.

---

## Why Use Multiple AI Systems?

Benefits:

* Compare answers
* Verify information
* Identify weaknesses
* Reduce errors

Never rely on a single source.

---

## Comparison Exercise

Ask both ChatGPT and Claude:

What are the biggest opportunities and risks of AI in higher education?

Compare:

* Detail
* Clarity
* Organization
* Evidence

---

## Strengths of Claude

Claude is often strong at:

* Long documents
* Summaries
* Writing assistance
* Structured analysis

---

## Strengths of ChatGPT

ChatGPT is often strong at:

* Brainstorming
* Interactive conversations
* Creative problem solving
* Broad topic coverage

---

## Best Practice

Use both tools when:

* Making important decisions
* Conducting research
* Comparing viewpoints

If both systems agree and reliable sources support the answer, confidence increases.

---

## First Exercise

Choose a topic you care about.

Ask ChatGPT and Claude the same question.

Compare results.

Record observations.
