# Bootcamp Glossary

## AI

Artificial Intelligence.

Software that can generate content, analyze information, and assist with problem solving.

---

## Prompt

The instructions given to an AI system.

---

## Hallucination

An AI-generated response that is incorrect or fabricated.

---

## Repository

A project folder managed through GitHub.

---

## Commit

A saved version of project changes.

---

## Verification

Checking information using independent sources.

---

## Workflow

A repeatable process for completing a task.

---

## Automation

Reducing repetitive manual work.

---

## Analysis

Turning information into insights.

---

## Recommendation

A suggested action based on evidence.

---

## Dashboard

A summary view of important information.

---

## Agent

A specialized AI assistant designed to perform a specific role.

---

## Data

Information collected for analysis.

---

## Bias

A tendency toward a particular perspective or outcome.

---

## Confidence Level

An estimate of how reliable a conclusion is.

---

## Productivity

Completing valuable work efficiently and effectively.
