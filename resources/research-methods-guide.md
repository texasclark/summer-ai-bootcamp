# Research Methods Guide

## What Is Research?

Research is the process of gathering information to answer questions and make decisions.

---

# The Research Process

1. Define the Problem
2. Gather Information
3. Evaluate Sources
4. Analyze Findings
5. Make Recommendations

---

# Good Research Questions

Examples:

What factors influence game popularity?

What makes a successful performance song?

What trends are emerging?

---

# Source Types

Primary Sources

Original information.

Examples:

* Official announcements
* Interviews
* Surveys

Secondary Sources

Analysis of information.

Examples:

* News articles
* Reviews
* Reports

---

# Research Bias

All sources contain bias.

Ask:

What perspective is represented?

What perspective is missing?

---

# Evidence-Based Recommendations

Good recommendations:

* Use evidence
* Explain reasoning
* Acknowledge uncertainty

Bad recommendations:

* Rely on assumptions
* Ignore conflicting information

---
