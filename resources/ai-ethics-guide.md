# AI Ethics Guide

## Purpose

AI is powerful.

Powerful tools should be used responsibly.

---

# Ethical Principles

## Honesty

Do not present AI-generated information as verified fact.

---

## Transparency

Be clear when AI was used.

---

## Responsibility

You are responsible for final decisions.

Not the AI.

---

## Privacy

Do not upload:

* Passwords
* Sensitive personal information
* Private records

---

# Academic Integrity

AI can assist learning.

AI should not replace learning.

Always follow course policies.

---

# Bias

AI systems may reflect biases present in training data.

Consider:

* Missing perspectives
* Cultural assumptions
* Data limitations

---

# Human Judgment

AI should assist decisions.

Humans should make decisions.

Always apply critical thinking.
