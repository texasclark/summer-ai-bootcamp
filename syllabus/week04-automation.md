# Week 4 – Automation

## Objective

Use AI to automate repetitive information gathering and reporting.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Students will:

* Build reusable prompts
* Create repeatable workflows
* Understand automation concepts
* Save time through AI-assisted processes

---

## What is Automation?

Automation means reducing repetitive work.

Examples:

* Weekly reports
* Research summaries
* Monitoring updates
* Tracking trends

---

## Nate Project

### Weekly Horror Intelligence Brief

Report Includes:

* New releases
* Game Pass updates
* Trending titles
* Community discussions

Output:

Top recommendations for the week.

---

## Alaina Project

### Weekly K-Pop Trend Brief

Report Includes:

* New releases
* Viral performances
* Spotify growth
* Audience engagement

Output:

Top songs to evaluate.

---

## Reusable Prompt Systems

Goal:

Create prompts that can be used every week with minimal changes.

---

## Deliverable

Semi-Automated Research Workflow

Document:

* Inputs
* Process
* Outputs
* Time saved

---

## Success Criteria

Student can:

* Repeat workflow easily
* Produce consistent results
* Explain the process
