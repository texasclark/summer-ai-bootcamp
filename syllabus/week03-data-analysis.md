# Week 3 – Data Collection and Analysis

## Objective

Learn how information is collected, organized, and evaluated.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Students will:

* Understand structured data
* Understand unstructured data
* Analyze datasets with AI
* Identify bias and quality issues
* Create meaningful summaries

---

## Topics

### Structured Data

Examples:

* Spreadsheets
* Tables
* Databases

Structured data is organized and easy to analyze.

---

### Unstructured Data

Examples:

* Reviews
* Articles
* Videos
* Social media posts

Most online information is unstructured.

---

### Data Quality

Questions to ask:

* Is it accurate?
* Is it current?
* Is it complete?
* Is it biased?

---

### AI-Assisted Analysis

AI can:

* Identify trends
* Summarize findings
* Create categories
* Highlight anomalies

---

## Exercise

Gather data related to your project.

Examples:

Nate:

* Steam review scores
* Metacritic ratings

Alaina:

* Spotify streams
* YouTube views

Analyze findings using AI.

---

## Deliverable

Enhanced Recommendation System

Include:

* Data sources
* Analysis
* Findings
* Updated rankings

---

## Success Criteria

Student can:

* Explain the data
* Defend conclusions
* Identify limitations
