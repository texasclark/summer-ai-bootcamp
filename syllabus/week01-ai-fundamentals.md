# Week 1 – AI Fundamentals

## Objective

Learn how to work effectively with AI tools.

The most valuable AI skill is not coding.

The most valuable AI skill is asking good questions.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Students will:

* Understand prompt engineering
* Understand hallucinations
* Verify AI-generated information
* Compare AI tools
* Build a research workflow

---

## Topics

### Prompt Engineering

Weak Prompt:

"Tell me about marketing."

Better Prompt:

"Act as a marketing analyst. Explain three current digital marketing trends for small businesses and provide examples."

---

### Context Matters

AI performs better when provided:

* Background
* Goals
* Constraints
* Desired output format

---

### Hallucinations

AI sometimes creates:

* Incorrect facts
* Fake citations
* False confidence

Never assume AI is correct.

Always verify.

---

### Verification Techniques

Verify information using:

* Multiple AI systems
* Search engines
* Official sources
* Industry publications

---

## Exercise

Research a topic of personal interest using:

* ChatGPT
* Claude
* Google Search
* YouTube

Compare:

* Accuracy
* Detail
* Speed
* Quality

---

## Deliverable

Research Report

Include:

* Topic
* Findings
* Sources
* Verification process
* Conclusions

---

## Success Criteria

Student demonstrates:

* Effective prompts
* Fact checking
* Independent verification
* Professional presentation
