# Week 6 – Capstone Project

## Objective

Build something genuinely useful.

The project should solve a real problem and continue to provide value after the bootcamp ends.

---

## Requirements

Project must:

* Solve a real problem
* Use AI effectively
* Include research
* Include analysis
* Include automation
* Be maintainable

---

## Capstone Structure

### Problem

What problem are you solving?

---

### Workflow

How does information move through the system?

---

### AI Usage

How was AI used?

Examples:

* Research
* Analysis
* Ranking
* Recommendations
* Reporting

---

### Results

What did the project accomplish?

---

### Lessons Learned

What worked?

What didn't?

What would you improve?

---

## Presentation Format

### Introduction

Problem being solved.

### Demonstration

Show workflow.

### Results

Present findings.

### Reflection

Discuss lessons learned.

---

## Deliverables

Repository containing:

* Documentation
* Prompts
* Workflows
* Reports
* Final presentation materials

---

## Success Criteria

The project:

* Produces useful output
* Saves time
* Demonstrates AI-assisted productivity
* Shows thoughtful decision-making

Remember:

The goal is not to write code.

The goal is to solve problems effectively using AI.
